# Always Move Forward — No Unnecessary Verification

Do not ask for permission or confirmation before making changes. Just do the work.

- Do NOT stop to ask "Should I go ahead?" — just go ahead
- Do NOT ask "Want me to commit?" — commit and push to `dev` automatically when a feature is done
- Do NOT present a plan and wait for approval — execute the plan
- Do NOT ask before creating files, editing files, or making changes — just make them
- The only time to ask is when the task itself is genuinely ambiguous and you need clarification on *what* to build, not *whether* to build it

The user wants momentum. Ship it, explain what you did, and move on.
